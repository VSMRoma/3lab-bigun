The sea whispers secrets older than time itself, its waves crashing against ancient stones that have stood sentinel through the centuries. In that vast, boundless darkness, currents carry whispers of gods and monsters, tales of heroes long gone, and civilizations lost to the tide. It’s a haunting reminder of our place in the cosmos—a delicate balance, where every drop tells a story, and every wave speaks of mysteries beyond mortal comprehension.
